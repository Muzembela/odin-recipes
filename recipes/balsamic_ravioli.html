Description

Balsamic ravioli is a sophisticated yet simple Italian dish consisting of ravioli (usually cheese-filled) tossed in a sauce of browned butter and reduced balsamic vinegar. The combination creates a sweet and sour, caramelized, and rich flavor. Is often finished with walnuts and Parmesan cheese for added texture.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup walnuts
  • 1 (25 ounce) package store-bought ravioli, any flavor
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Step 1

    • Toast walnuts in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until browned and fragrant, 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from skillet.

  • Step 2

    • Fill a pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in ravioli and return to a boil. Cook uncovered, occasionally stirring, until ravioli float to the top and filling is hot, 3 to 5 minutes.

  • Step 3

    • Drain Warm butter in a skillet over medium heat until slightly brown, about 1 minute. Add balsamic vinegar,cook and stir until melted and combined, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in ravioli, mix until combined. Top with walnuts and Parmesan cheese.
